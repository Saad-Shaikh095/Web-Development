Shah Rukh Khan

The King of Bollywood

About

Shah Rukh Khan, also known as the King Khan, is one of the most popular and influential actors in the world. He has appeared in more than 80 Bollywood films and earned numerous accolades.

Achievements

  • 14 Filmfare Awards
  • Padma Shri by the Government of India
  • Numerous international honors
  • Iconic roles in movies like Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge, My Name is Khan, and Chennai Express.
